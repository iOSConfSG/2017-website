Jorge D. Ortiz


@jdortiz

Jorge worked for HP for more than 15 years, where he had the privilege to perform all kinds of tasks related to information security (penetration testing, incident handling, in security depth analysis, etc.) with the best profesionals in the field.

For last 8 years he has been working in different aspects of mobile development. After playing with PalmOS, he learned Android programming for the first Google App contest. Immediately after, he started to play with the first iPhone SDK.
