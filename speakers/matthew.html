Matthew's Talk on Day 1 - 10:00 a.m. - 10:35 a.m.

Building Composable Interfaces in Swift

Swift is a relatively new language and there is yet not many established practices for using its features. This talk attempts to explore and push the boundaries of protocols and see where this can take us.

Having worked with Objective-C for nearly 8 years and dabbling with Swift since its introduction, Matthew hopes to share this with other developers in the community.
