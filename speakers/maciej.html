Maciej Piotrowski


@paciej00

Maciej is an Electronics and Telecommunications graduate with passion for mobile applications and 5 years of experience in iOS development. His professional experience includes projects related with satellite navigation, iBeacons and Bluetooth Low Energy communications. He is also a blogger and a conference/meetups speaker.

Maciej's Talk on Day 2 - 04:45 p.m. - 05:20 p.m.

Locating the Yeti with iOS

CoreLocation and satellite navigation systems are not the only way to obtain one’s location. In some scenarios it is suitable to benefit from CoreTelephony and CoreMotion. This talk overviews iOS 11 location services and shows a concept of positioning people by using iPhone’s motion coprocessor.
